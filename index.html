Karoliina Lohiniva

karoliina.lohiniva<at>unige.ch

I am currently a visiting scholar at New York University, where I conduct postdoctoral research funded by the Swiss National Science Foundation. I received my PhD (2018) and MA (2014) in linguistics and my BA (2012) in linguistics and French as a foreign language from the University of Geneva, Switzerland, and was previusly a visiting scholar at the University of Delaware (2017-2018).

My research interests include focus-sensitivity, case, wh-phenomena, alternatives, and dynamic models of discourse. Most of my work is focused on Finnish.
